## Service packages (proposal-ready)

### 1) Starter Validation Sprint

- Goal: prove value quickly with one high-impact workflow.
- Includes: setup, first scraper/bot flow, and clear handover notes.
- Typical budget: USD 150-350.

### 2) Growth Automation Build

- Goal: expand to a production-ready scraping and pipeline layer.
- Includes: extraction logic, data cleanup, delivery outputs, and reliability hardening.
- Typical budget: USD 400-1,200.

### 3) Ongoing Optimization Retainer

- Goal: continuously improve reliability and business usefulness.
- Includes: iterative tuning, source updates, report improvements, and support cadence.
- Typical budget: USD 100-500 per week.

## Discovery-call questions that sound human and strategic

1. Which manual process is consuming the most time each week?
2. Which decisions are currently delayed because data arrives too late?
3. Where should output be delivered: Telegram, CSV, Sheets, API, or email?
4. What update frequency is needed for the workflow to be useful?
5. What would make this project feel like a clear win in the first 14 days?

---

## Proposal language patterns (high-conversion tone)

- "I recommend starting with 5 high-impact items to validate outcomes before scaling."
- "I will prioritize decision-ready alerts, not just raw scraped values."
- "The first milestone is designed to show practical business value in days, not weeks."
- "After validation, we can expand coverage without disrupting your current workflow."

---

## Avoid in international proposals

- vague technical-only promises with no business metric;
- overly generic AI-style wording with no operational context;
- references to "doing everything" before a scoped first milestone;
- non-English snippets in client-facing documentation.
