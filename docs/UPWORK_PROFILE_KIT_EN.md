# Upwork Profile Kit (English-First, Client-Oriented)

Use this file as a reusable source when updating your Upwork profile,
proposals, and introductory messages.

---

## Suggested profile headline

**Python Automation Specialist | Price Intelligence, Web Data Monitoring, and Client-Ready Delivery**

---

## Suggested profile overview

I help ecommerce and procurement teams automate repetitive monitoring tasks so
they can make faster, data-backed decisions.

My projects are built around business impact first:

- reduced manual effort;
- faster reaction to pricing opportunities;
- clear operational visibility for non-technical stakeholders.

I deliver practical Python automation with clean documentation, scoped rollouts,
and communication that clients can trust.

Typical engagement flow:

1. map the highest-impact use case;
2. deliver a small but measurable first milestone;
3. optimize based on real operating feedback.

---

## Service packages (proposal-ready)

### 1) Starter Validation Sprint

- Goal: prove value quickly with a limited SKU/sample scope.
- Includes: setup, parsing rules, initial alerts, and handover notes.
- Typical budget: USD 150-350.

### 2) Growth Automation Build

- Goal: expand to a larger catalog and harden reliability.
- Includes: selector tuning, monitoring cycles, and operational playbook.
- Typical budget: USD 400-1,000.

### 3) Ongoing Optimization Retainer

- Goal: continuously improve signal quality and stakeholder usefulness.
- Includes: iterative tuning, reporting improvements, and support cadence.
- Typical budget: USD 100-500 per week.

## Discovery-call questions that sound human and strategic

1. Which decisions are currently delayed because data arrives too late?
2. Which product categories or suppliers create the biggest margin risk?
3. What alert noise level is acceptable for your team?
4. Who consumes the output: analyst, manager, or executive sponsor?
5. What would make this project feel like a clear win in the first 14 days?

---

## Proposal language patterns (high-conversion tone)

- "I recommend starting with 5 high-impact items to validate outcomes before scaling."
- "I will prioritize decision-ready alerts, not just raw scraped values."
- "The first milestone is designed to show practical business value in days, not weeks."
- "After validation, we can expand coverage without disrupting your current workflow."

---

## Avoid in international proposals

- vague technical-only promises with no business metric;
- overly generic AI-style wording with no operational context;
- references to "doing everything" before a scoped first milestone;
- non-English snippets in client-facing documentation.
