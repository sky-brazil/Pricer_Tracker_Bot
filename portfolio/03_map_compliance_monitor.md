# Project 03 - MAP Compliance Monitor

## Positioning
Monitoring workflow for brands and distributors that need to enforce MAP
(Minimum Advertised Price) compliance across resellers.

## Typical client profile
- brands with authorized and unauthorized reseller mix;
- legal/commercial teams dealing with recurring MAP violations;
- operations needing proof and time-stamped evidence.

## Business problem
"We know some sellers are breaking MAP, but we cannot track consistently."

## Delivery scope
1. Catalog and seller page mapping;
2. MAP threshold rules by product family;
3. Violation alerts with URLs and observed prices;
4. Evidence-ready logs for follow-up actions.

## KPI goals
- improved MAP policy visibility;
- faster identification of repeat violators;
- better support for commercial and legal enforcement.

## Differentiator
Compliance orientation: output is structured to support escalation workflow,
not only tactical buying decisions.

## Upwork budget framing (typical range)
- Starter: USD 250-500
- Growth: USD 600-1,400
- Compliance maintenance: USD 150-450/week

## Human touch in communication
Discovery starts with policy context, not code. Ask how the client currently
handles violations and design alerts to match that process exactly.
