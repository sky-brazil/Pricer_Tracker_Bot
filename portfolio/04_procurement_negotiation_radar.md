# Project 04 - Procurement Negotiation Radar

## Positioning
B2B sourcing intelligence for procurement teams that need external price
references before supplier negotiation rounds.

## Typical client profile
- importers and distributors with recurring purchase cycles;
- procurement managers negotiating quarterly contracts;
- teams with low visibility across alternative suppliers.

## Business problem
"We negotiate with limited external reference data and lose leverage."

## Delivery scope
1. Priority SKU monitoring across selected supplier channels;
2. Threshold alerts tied to negotiation targets;
3. Export-ready records for negotiation meetings;
4. Executive summary notes (optional add-on).

## KPI goals
- stronger negotiation baseline;
- better timing for purchase orders;
- reduced overpayment risk on critical items.

## Differentiator
This profile is decision-support oriented: alert logic is built around contract
timelines and procurement planning, not ecommerce campaign speed.

## Upwork budget framing (typical range)
- Starter: USD 300-650
- Growth: USD 700-1,800
- Procurement support retainer: USD 200-500/week

## Human touch in communication
Start with one upcoming supplier negotiation and design the first alert bundle
around that exact meeting. Clients feel value immediately when context is real.
