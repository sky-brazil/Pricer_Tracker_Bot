# Project 02 - Flash Deals Scout

## Positioning
Real-time deal detection for ecommerce teams where speed is the competitive
advantage (fashion drops, seasonal inventory, promo windows).

## Typical client profile
- lean operations that cannot monitor hundreds of pages manually;
- campaigns where a 2-hour delay can kill conversion potential;
- heavy reliance on short-term paid traffic.

## Business problem
"We discover great deals too late, after the best inventory is gone."

## Delivery scope
1. Fast-refresh monitoring cycles (15 to 60 minutes);
2. Product-level selectors for mixed storefront structures;
3. Discount-hit notifications with priority labels;
4. Optional follow-up recommendations in alert text.

## KPI goals
- faster response to temporary discounts;
- improved campaign timing;
- less budget waste on expired opportunities.

## Differentiator
Designed for tactical speed: this profile favors rapid signal detection and
action-ready alerts over heavy reporting layers.

## Upwork budget framing (typical range)
- Starter: USD 120-280
- Growth: USD 300-700
- Rapid iteration support: USD 80-220/week

## Human touch in communication
Use a "war room" style rollout in week 1: monitor only top campaign items,
collect feedback daily, then expand based on what the team actually uses.
