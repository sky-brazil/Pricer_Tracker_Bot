# Project 01 - Marketplace Guardian

## Positioning
Automation project for marketplace sellers who lose margin because competitor
prices change faster than their team can react.

## Typical client profile
- 200+ SKUs with frequent repricing pressure;
- 1 to 3 people managing pricing manually;
- operation in Amazon, Walmart, and niche marketplaces.

## Business problem
"We are checking competitor prices in spreadsheets and always reacting late."

## Delivery scope
1. Product list onboarding (CSV or Google Sheet export);
2. Target-price rules by category;
3. Monitoring scheduler (hourly or custom interval);
4. Telegram alerts with direct SKU and URL references;
5. Handover doc for internal team usage.

## KPI goals
- 60% to 80% reduction in manual checks;
- faster repricing reaction window;
- clearer visibility of below-threshold opportunities.

## Differentiator
Built with a margin-first logic: alerts prioritize products where delayed action
has the biggest gross-profit impact.

## Upwork budget framing (typical range)
- Starter: USD 150-350
- Growth: USD 400-900
- Ongoing optimization: USD 100-300/week

## Human touch in communication
During discovery, ask for 5 high-impact SKUs first. This creates quick wins and
shows the client practical value before scaling to the full catalog.
